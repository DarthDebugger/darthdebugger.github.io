About

The Prismatic Shard is built upon the ATTiny1616 Microcontroller. This AVR series 1 chip uses very little power and has fantastic capabilities and IO for its size. However, the flash available is limited to 16kb. This is leaps and bounds better than its cousins like the ATTiny85 (2kb) or the ATTiny13 (1kb) but in order to get the most out of the badge, we have separated some of the story telling elements for the badge and hosted them here instead where we are not bound by the flash size limitations and can share a richer experience.

As you progress through the adventure and solve challenges, you will enjoy a rich backstory / choose your own adventure and maybe even obtain a few codes that can be entered into the serial interface on the badge to unlock features and help progress with the badge.
